Babotie

Description

Babotie is a traditional South African curry dish, topped with an egg custard. It is a ground beef curry that is sweeter in flavor than typical curry dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 25g butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1kg lean ground beef
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 3 cloves
  • 5 allspice berries
  • 2 tbsp peach chutney
  • 2 tbsp apricot jam
  • 3 tbsp sultanas
  • 6 bay leaves

    • For the topping

  • 300 ml full-cream milk
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 F.
  2. Put the bread in a shallow dish and poar milk over. Set aside to soak.
  3. Fry the onions in the butter until soft and starting to color. Add the garlic and beef, stirring well. Break up the ground beaf in to small pieces while cooking until just browned. Stir in the curry, herbs, spices, chutney, jam, sultanas and 2 bay leaves with 1 tsp salt and ground black pepper to taste.
  4. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Squeeze milk from bread and set milk aside. Mix in to the meat mixture until well blended. Transfer in to an oven proof 12x8" dish. Press the mixture down well and smooth the top.
  5. For the topping, beat milk and eggs with seasoning and then pour over meat. Top with remaining bay leaves and back for 35-40 min, until top is golden brown.
  6. Serve with yellow rice, shredded coconut, sliced banana and chutney.
